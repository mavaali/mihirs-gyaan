Step 1 · your first win (no setup)

Run the editing loop on a draft you have

Copy the skill, open a chat, paste it with any draft — an email, a paragraph, a post. Watch it score your writing, change one thing, and keep or revert based on whether the score went up.

Open Claude Open ChatGPT

After you paste, send: "Follow this SKILL.md, use the rubric, run in chat mode on my draft, one experiment at a time." The first reply is a score table. Then it runs experiments — and you'll see it revert a change that doesn't help. That's the moment.

The bundled rubric is calibrated to one person's blog voice — great for seeing the loop work. Later, build your own for your genre (that's the capstone below).
